The first interaction you play will be for practice and will not be part of your possible earnings in this experiment. One of the subsequent 3 interactions will be picked at random and the results from that interaction will determine your additional earnings for your participation in this experiment. An interaction must consist of at least five rounds to be eligible for payment.

Payoffs for decisions made in an interaction are as follows:

  • For each round of the interaction in which you modify your model of your partner $0.05 will be deducted from your total payoff.
  • Five rounds of the interaction will be picked at random for additional earnings:
    • For each of those rounds that results in a disagreement, you will receive nothing.
    • For each of those rounds that results in an agreement, your payoff will depend on how much you personally like the agreed upon option. The table below summarizes all possible payoffs you could earn in a round based on what the agreed upon option is*:
