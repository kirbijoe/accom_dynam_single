Your Model of Your Partner

For each of the four partner interactions:

  • The interaction will consist of multiple rounds.
  • You will be shown a scale with some labels in black and some in red.
  • The black labels will represent your model of your own preferences. They are fixed and cannot be changed.
  • The red labels will represent your model of your partner's preferences. They can be adjusted at any time.

An example using sports is shown to the right. This example is only for demonstration purposes. Click on some of the red labels on this example and drag them up (to increase liking) or down (to decrease liking) to see how the scale changes.
