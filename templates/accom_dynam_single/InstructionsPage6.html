Depending on whether you and your partner agree/disagree , you may want to modify the placements of the red labels on your model of your partner. However, you will have a cost for each round in which you make modifications.

The goal is for both of you to try to agree on which option is "best" for many pairings of items.

The interaction will end when you and your partner agree for 10 consecutive rounds or the timer runs out.

*This is an animation. It is not interactive.

For example, based on results from previous rounds suppose you think that your partner likes tennis more than what your current model implies. Then, as in the graphic to the left, you could update your model of your partner by clicking on the "Tennis" label and dragging it up.
